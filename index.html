About Me

Hi! I'm Chloe Hodgins, a multidisciplinary product and user interface designer with a background in robotic design and coding. As I continued to grow my passion for art and graphic design I unlocked another interest in robotics and tech, leading to winning a robot design competition. Exposure to health care devices led to my design philosophy: Design made for people should be designed for people.

Today, I'm pursuing an Honours Bachelor degree in Global Business and Digital Arts at Waterloo University to fulfill my dream of designing medical device screens that put users first.
